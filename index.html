Nutrition & Fuel
▾
🍌
Before Training
Banana or half cup oats 30–60 min before. Fast carbs = energy and performance.
🥛
After Training
Whey protein shake within 30–60 min post-session. Most important protein window of the day.
🎯
Daily Protein
1.8–2.2g per kg of bodyweight. Non-negotiable for recomp. Chicken, eggs, Greek yogurt, whey.
📉
Calories
300–400 kcal below your TDEE daily. Enough to lose fat without losing muscle.
💧
Hydration
3–4 liters of water daily. More on training days.
